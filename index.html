Why this exists

Modern IT can feel unclear from both sides.

Individuals are often told to “get into tech” without seeing what the work actually looks like: the tickets, tools, access requests, documentation, troubleshooting, and everyday decisions.

Growing teams often build IT processes reactively until onboarding, offboarding, access, and SaaS ownership become messy, manual, or inconsistent.

Modern IT Pathways exists to make those systems easier to understand and easier to improve, without overcomplicating the first step.
